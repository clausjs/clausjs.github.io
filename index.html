About Joseph

Joseph Claus has experience with a variety of frontend and backend applications (primarily working with Javascript and ReactJS for most of his career). Located in Charlottesville Virginia, Joseph enjoys learning new languages and experimenting with different tools for getting the job done. The best challenges are the ones that require learning new skills.

Discord Community Mod/Owner

Joseph is a Discord member/owner/mod of a community designed to be inviting to gamers. A collection of people who enjoy video games and are looking for other like minded individuals to play multiplayer games with or just to chat while playing single player games. The community also includes general text chatting channels primarily for random discussion of things outside of gaming.

The community is patrolled by a bot (Joe_Bot) which is also written and maintained by Joseph. The website which advertises the Discord is also written by Joseph as a single-page application written in Javascript/ReactJS using React-Router.
